Understanding Kidney Damage Progression

The 3D model demonstrates how chronic kidney disease affects kidney structure:

  • Normal Kidney: Healthy structure with smooth surface
  • Stage 1-2: Minimal visible changes, normal size
  • Stage 3: Early scarring, slight size reduction
  • Stage 4: Significant scarring, noticeable shrinkage
  • Stage 5 (ESRD): Severe scarring, small irregular kidneys with cyst formation

Note: In a complete implementation, each stage would show accurate anatomical changes.
